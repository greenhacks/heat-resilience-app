Next Steps

1. Have conversations.
Conversation and communication increase resilience because they sow seeds for preparedness and action.

Try these conversation starters:
"Have you noticed that summers have started earlier and have gotten hotter?"

"How do you feel about the changing climate?"

"How prepared do you feel to handle extreme heat? Is there some action we can take together?"

2. Check out urban cooling centers near you.
Cities like Los Angeles and Miami are recognizing the impacts of heat. Miami has even hired its first Chief Heat Officer in 2021!
You can learn more about solutions for urban heat here.

3. Understand the impacts of heat.
Check out the non-profit's Climate Resolve's Rx for Hot Cities.
