FAQs

1) Why make this app?
Heat is increasingly catching people unprepared in places such as the Pacific Northwest where extreme high temperatures are not the norm.

Air conditioning is also not readily available always.


2) Why use the heat index instead of ambient temperature as a measure of thermal comfort?
The heat index is more accurate because it takes humidity into account. Humidity can make the temperature feel hotter.

You can find more information in this New York Times article: What Is the Heat Index, and Why Does It Matter?"
